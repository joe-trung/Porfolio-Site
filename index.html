About Me

Trung Tran is a dedicated and passionate Data Engineer. While Trung's background in IT may not have been extensive, his unwavering passion for data engineering and commitment to becoming a lifelong learner have driven him to excel in the field.

Trung has bolstered his skills with an AWS Cloud Practitioner Certification and multiple certificates from Coursera. These certifications cover a wide range of IT skills, including Python, Flask, Pandas, PySparks, Kafka, Docker, Kubernetes, Matplotlib, Plotly, Seaborn, SQL, Machine Learning models, Postman API, big data skills and R language.

With an MBA from the University of Houston - Victoria, Trung brings a wealth of business experience as a business owner and partner in various industries, such as cosmetology, real estate, and logistics. These experiences have instilled in him a sense of entrepreneurship, effective time management, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities.

Trung's passion for data engineering knows no bounds. He consistently seeks opportunities to expand his skill set. Currently, he is focusing on broadening his expertise in front-end development and machine learning, including Angular, Pytorch, and TensorFlow.

AWS Cloud Practitioner

Link to certificates

While attending the Zip Code Wilmington Boot Camp to become a Data Engineer, I had the opportunity to meet a senior manager at JPMorgan Chase. He had recently obtained an AWS certification and recommended that I pursue it as well. Balancing the rigorous 1000-hour program at Zip Code within just 12 weeks was challenging, but I was determined to succeed. Despite the demanding schedule, I managed to allocate an additional 300 hours to complete an AWS course and successfully passed the certification exam on my first attempt. I'm extremely grateful that I made the decision to pursue it, as AWS became an integral part of almost all my projects thereafter.

Learning on Coursera

Link to certificates

Transitioning into the technology field after more than a decade as a small business owner, I initially felt unsure about where to start. However, discovering Coursera proved to be a life-saving solution for me. The value of the subscription was undeniable. It provided me with the opportunity to learn extensively, earn numerous certificates, and, most importantly, showcased my ability to absorb knowledge at a rapid pace. The flexibility to learn anything, anytime, and at my own preferred speed was truly empowering. There were instances where I completed four weeks' worth of coursework in just two days, highlighting my passion and dedication for continuous learning. Coursera has become a treasured platform for me, and I truly cherish the learning experiences it offers.
